# **App Name**: PerformanceLeads

## Core Features:

- Lead Generation: Generate qualified leads through various digital channels, focusing on performance-based pricing.
- AI-Powered Ad Copy Generation: Leverage AI to automatically generate compelling ad copy optimized for conversions. The AI will use data and insights as a tool, in determining ad performance.
- ROI Tracking Dashboard: Provide a real-time dashboard for clients to track ROI and marketing spend.
- Performance-Based Pricing: Offer a flexible pricing model based on the number of qualified leads generated.
- Automated Lead Scoring: Use AI to score leads based on their likelihood to convert into customers.
- MSME Segmentation: Categorize MSMEs based on revenue and other relevant metrics.
- Landing page content creation: Generate content to create better landing pages based on industry using generative AI

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Orange (#FF6600) for energy and performance.
- Background color: Light gray (#F7F9FC) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Electric Green (#00D9A3) for highlighting growth and success.
- Body and headline font: 'Poppins', a bold sans-serif, for a contemporary and precise feel.
- Use modern, flat, colorful icons to represent different services and features.
- Maintain a clean and modern layout with clear sections for different aspects of the website.
- Incorporate subtle animations, such as counting numbers and fade-ins, to enhance user experience.
